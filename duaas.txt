سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ
||
SubhanAllahi wa bihamdihi
||
Glory be to Allah and praise Him
---
اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ السَّلَامُ وَمِنْكَ السَّلَامُ
||
Allahumma antas-salaam wa minkas-salaam
||
O Allah, You are Peace and from You comes peace
---
لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ
||
La ilaha illallah wahdahu la sharika lah
||
There is no deity but Allah, alone without partner
